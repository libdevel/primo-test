How does this work?

Primo provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more.

Primo also helps you manage your research:

  • Renew books and media
  • Create favorites lists
  • Create citations
  • Search multiple library databases at once.

Primo search tips

Use phrase searching for an exact match
e.g. "gold mining" otago

Use this wildcard symbol (?) within a word to find single letter variations
e.g. wom?n - will find women and woman

Use the wildcard or truncation symbol (*) to find alternative word endings
e.g. mine* will find mines, miner, mineralogy, minerals...
